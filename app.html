How to use Monitor Preview

Follow these steps to preview and monitor your screen:
  1. Click the Start Stream button in the top right.
  2. Your browser will prompt you to choose what to share. Select the "Entire Screen" tab to capture the full monitor view.
  3. Once live, use the Picture-in-Picture (PiP) icon to pop the video out into a floating window that stays on top of other applications.
  4. Click Stop Stream when you're finished.
